About Me

Hello! I am Maleka Sultana, a passionate artist dedicated to expressing emotions and stories through my artwork. With a background in fine arts and years of creative exploration, my work ranges from vibrant paintings to thought-provoking installations. Each piece is a journey of color, texture, and imagination.
