Welcome to the Portfolio Website of Artist Maleka Sultana (Nupur)

Maleka Sultana Nupur, a painter from Jhenaidah Sadar, Bangladesh, holds a BSc and MSc in Zoology. She has been passionate about art since childhood and has won numerous national awards. She has recently showcased her work in exhibitions across Bangladesh, India, Nepal, and Sri Lanka. Maleka specializes in acrylic painting, mixed media, and dress design and is committed to continuous growth as an artist.
